About

// ======== WHO WE ARE ======== //

Mother Johnson is a band born out of the Amsterdam underground scene. Rooted in raw energy and an unfiltered approach to sound, the group blends noise, shoegaze, and post-punk into something dense, urgent, and unapologetically loud.

From the cramped stages of OCCII to the storied halls of Cinetol, Mother Johnson brings an intensity that pins you to the wall. The songs hit like transmissions from a broken radio — static-laced melodies fighting through walls of feedback, anchored by rhythms that refuse to let up.

The debut single “100 German Cities / Champagne (Happy for You)” captures the band at their most focused: two sides of the same restless coin. Arriving soon on all platforms.
